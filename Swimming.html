SWIMMING

About Swim

Prime Fitness Swim offers more than world-class pools. Our industry leading-instructors help those looking to learn to swim for the first time, or who want to improve their stroke to be more competitive.

Swim Programs


Private Lessons

One-on-one attention with one of our highly trained instructors. Customized to you or child’s personal needs, goals and confidence.

Kids Swim Lessons

Children love to swim, and it’s one of the most important lessons they can learn in life. Swimming lessons teach children self-confidence,and physical fitness.

Semi-Private Lessons

Bring a friend and hop in. Our semi-private lessons offer a 1:2 instructor-to-student ratio to help you improve your swimming quickly and efficiently together.

Group Swim Lessons

Become great in a group. Our 1:6 instructor-to-student ratio will help you improve your technique in a fun group environment.

Swim Teams

Open to all abilities and ages 5+, our Swim Team offers weekly practices, specialized training, regular competitions and the chance to win awards.

Kids Swim Clinic

When school’s on break, bring your kids to our Swim Clinic. Our 2 to 3 hour clinic is made for kids ages 5–12 and includes swim lessons, healthy meals, water safety skills and fun water sports.

Aquatics


Indoor Lap Pools

Get in the water and get moving. Our indoor lap pool provides the aquatic environment you need to refine your stroke or just enjoy a morning workout.

Indoor Water Slides & Leisure Pools

No matter what time of year, we've got an open, welcoming pool waiting for all.

Outdoor Lap Pools

Start your day with a morning swim or train for an event — all with refreshing, open air.

Outdoor Water Slides & Leisure Pools

Take in the sun, have lunch and have fun at our resort-like pools.

Indoor & Outdoor Whirlpools

Heated luxurious whirlpools to relax and recover in all year.

Zero-Depth Entry Pools

Gradually increases in depth to keep your little ones safe and in sight.
